Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who are Sahayathri Tour Planners?

We are a trusted tour operator based in Thrissur, Kerala, offering customized and cost-effective travel packages since 2008. We specialize in study tours, honeymoon trips, family vacations, and more.

2. What kind of tour packages do you offer?

We offer a wide range of packages including study tours for schools and colleges, honeymoon packages, family getaways, pilgrimage tours, and customized group tours across India.

3. How do I book a tour with you?

You can contact us via phone, WhatsApp, or email to discuss your requirements. Once we finalize your package, a small advance payment confirms your booking.

4. Do you provide transportation and accommodation?

Yes, all our packages include comfortable transportation and verified hotel stays. We ensure clean, safe, and conveniently located accommodations for all travelers.

5. What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation charges vary based on how many days before the trip you cancel. Please refer to our, Terms & Conditionssection for detailed information.

6. How long have you been in the travel business?

We have proudly been serving travelers since 2008 and have successfully organized hundreds of memorable tours across India.
