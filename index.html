About Us

Ready For Unforgettable Travel, Remember Us!

Sahayathri Tour Planners was born 16 years ago with a driven passion for travelling all over the world. We are located in Irinjalakuda, Thrissur, Kerala. Sahayathri Tour Planners is the idea of a single mind who is a passionate traveller, a strong independent woman, Minija Gopi. From the first trip itself she knows that she will step her foot in every part of the world and she will be a "Sahayathri"(co-traveller) for thousands of people to travel across the world.

For the last 16 years Sahayathri Tour Planners conducted over thousands of trips to various places including International, National and famous pilgrimages. We provide affordable packages, the best facilities, a comfortable journey and forever memories. The motto of Sahayathri Tour Planners is the happiness of our co-travellers and we fullfill that in every journey, which made them join us again and again in our upcoming trips.
