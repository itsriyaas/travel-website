Bhubaneswar – Puri – Kolkata Tour Itinerary

Destinations: Bhubaneswar, Puri & Kolkata
Duration: 05 Days / 04 Nights

Day 01: Bhubaneswar

  • Arrival at Bhubaneswar Airport/Railway Station
  • Pickup and transfer to hotel by reserved coach
  • Sightseeing: Nandankanan Zoo, Lingaraja Temple, Raja Rani Temple, Khandagiri & Udayagiri Caves
  • Overnight stay at hotel in Bhubaneswar

Day 02: Konark & Puri

  • Checkout from hotel and proceed to Konark
  • En-route visit Dhauli Shanti Stupa at Dhauligiri Hills
  • Visit Konark Sun Temple & Chandrabhaga Beach
  • Overnight stay at hotel in Puri

Day 03: Chilika Lake & Jagannath Temple

  • Checkout from hotel and visit Chilika Lake (Boating included)
  • Return to Puri and visit Jagannath Temple
  • Board overnight train to Kolkata at 10:00 PM (Train fare not included)

Day 04: Kolkata Sightseeing

  • Arrive in Kolkata and transfer to hotel (Early check-in subject to availability)
  • Sightseeing: Victoria Memorial, Mother Teresa’s House, Netaji Bhavan, Indian Museum, New Market
  • Experience Underground Metro Train Journey
  • Overnight stay at hotel in Kolkata

Day 05: Departure Day

  • Checkout from hotel
  • Local sightseeing: Kalighat Temple, Belur Math, Howrah Bridge
  • Drop at Kolkata Airport/Railway Station by evening
