Assam & Meghalaya Tour Itinerary

Destination: Assam & Meghalaya
Duration: 08 Days / 07 Nights
Coach: AC Tempo Traveller

Day 01: Arrival & Transfer to Shillong

  • Pick-up from Guwahati Airport/Railway Station
  • Transfer to Shillong by reserved coach
  • Check-in and overnight stay at hotel in Shillong

Meal Plan: Dinner only

Day 02: Shillong Local Sightseeing

  • Don Bosco Museum
  • Golf Course
  • Ward's Lake
  • Free time for shopping at Police Bazar

Overnight stay at hotel in Shillong.
Meal Plan: Breakfast & Dinner only

Day 03: Shillong – Cherrapunji – Shillong

  • Elephanta Falls
  • Duwan Sing Syiem View Point
  • Nohkalikai Falls
  • Mawsmai Caves
  • Seven Sister Falls

Return to Shillong. Dinner and overnight stay.
Meal Plan: Breakfast & Dinner only

Day 04: Shillong – Mawlynnong – Dawki – Shillong

  • Visit Asia's Cleanest Village: Mawlynnong
  • See the Living Root Bridge
  • Boating at Dawki Lake (near Bangladesh border)

Return to Shillong. Dinner and overnight stay.
Meal Plan: Breakfast & Dinner only

Day 05: Shillong – Kaziranga

  • Checkout after breakfast
  • Drive to Kaziranga (6–7 hours)
  • Visit Umiam Lake en route
  • Check-in at resort in Kaziranga

Dinner and overnight stay at Kaziranga.
Meal Plan: Breakfast & Dinner only

Day 06: Kaziranga Safari & Transfer to Guwahati

  • Early morning Elephant Safari (optional, own cost)
  • Jeep Safari (optional, own cost)
  • Return for breakfast and check-out
  • Transfer to Guwahati
  • Free time for shopping

Overnight stay at Guwahati.
Meal Plan: Breakfast & Dinner only

Day 07: Kamakhya Temple & Brahmaputra River Cruise

  • Visit Kamakhya Temple early morning
  • Optional sunset cruise on the Brahmaputra River (own cost & subject to availability)

Overnight stay at Guwahati.
Meal Plan: Breakfast & Dinner only

Day 08: Departure

  • Checkout from hotel
  • Transfer to airport or railway station

Meal Plan: Breakfast only

Tour Cost Includes

  • All transportation by AC Tempo Traveller
  • Meal Plan: MAP (Breakfast & Dinner)
  • All sightseeing and entry tickets as per itinerary
  • All permits, tolls, parking, and applicable taxes
  • Accommodation at Guwahati (2N), Shillong (4N), Kaziranga (1N) in 3-star hotels/resorts
  • Double sharing AC rooms
  • Authorized guide throughout the trip
  • One bottle of mineral water per person daily (on sightseeing days)

Tour Cost Excludes

  • GST @ 5%
  • Meals not mentioned in the inclusions
  • Boating at Dawki Lake
  • Boating at Brahmaputra River
  • Elephant/Jeep Safari at Kaziranga
  • Any service not explicitly mentioned in the inclusions
