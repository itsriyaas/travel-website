Vadodara – Ahmedabad – Bhavnagar – Gir – Dwaraka Tour Itinerary

Destinations: Vadodara – Ahmedabad – Bhavnagar – Gir – Dwaraka
Duration: 07 Days / 06 Nights

Day 01: Arrival & Transfer to Ahmedabad

  • Pickup from Vadodara Airport in the morning
  • Visit the Statue of Unity (Patel Statue)
  • Transfer to Ahmedabad
  • Overnight stay in Ahmedabad

Day 02: Ahmedabad Sightseeing & Transfer to Bhavnagar

  • Local sightseeing in Ahmedabad
  • Evening transfer to Bhavnagar
  • Overnight stay in Bhavnagar

Day 03: Nishkalank Mahadev Temple & Transfer to Somnath

  • Visit Nishkalankeswar Temple (temple in the sea)
  • Transfer to Somnath
  • Overnight stay in Somnath

Day 04: Gir National Park

  • Visit Gir National Park (Safari optional)
  • Return and overnight stay in Somnath

Day 05: Somnath Sightseeing & Transfer to Dwaraka

  • Local sightseeing in Somnath
  • Transfer to Dwaraka
  • Overnight stay in Dwaraka

Day 06: Full Day Dwaraka Sightseeing

  • Local sightseeing in and around Dwaraka
  • Overnight stay in Dwaraka

Day 07: Departure

  • Drop at Dwaraka Railway Station in the morning
