Day 01

Flight (IndiGo):

  • COK 06:05 → BOM 08:10
  • BOM 11:15 → KTM 14:05

The Temple City - Kathmandu

The tour group assembles at Kathmandu Airport by afternoon (2:00 PM).

In the evening, visit the famous Pashupatinath Temple.

Overnight stay at hotel in Kathmandu.

Day 02

Everest Mountain View Flight (optional, extra cost)

Full day sightseeing in Kathmandu including:

  • Boudhanath Temple
  • Budhanilkantha (Sleeping Vishnu)
  • Swayambhunath Buddhist Monastery
  • Kathmandu Durbar Square

Evening free for personal activities like shopping.

Overnight stay at hotel in Kathmandu.

Day 03

Kathmandu – Manakamana – Pokhara (200 kms / approx. 5 hrs)

After breakfast, transfer to Pokhara.

En route, visit Manakamana Temple via one of Asia’s largest cable cars.

Overnight stay at hotel in Pokhara.

Day 04

The Land of Lakes – Pokhara

Full day sightseeing in Pokhara including:

  • Boating to Tal Barahi Temple at Fewa Lake
  • Devi’s Waterfalls
  • Seti River
  • Sri Gupteshwar Mahadev Cave Temple
  • Gurkha Museum

Evening free for personal activities.

Overnight stay at hotel in Pokhara.

Day 05

Pokhara – Lumbini – Bhairava

Early morning trekking to Sarangkot Sunrise Point and return.

Checkout from hotel and proceed to Bhairava.

En route, visit the birthplace of Lord Buddha – Lumbini.

Overnight stay at hotel in Bhairava.

Day 06

After breakfast, drop at Gorakhpur Airport.

Resume journey to Gorakhpur (250 kms / approx. 6 hrs).
