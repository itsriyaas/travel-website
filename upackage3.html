Lakshadweep Tour Itinerary


Day 1:
Arrival and Agatti Island Sightseeing

  • Morning: Arrival at Agatti Airport. Welcome and transfer to accommodation. Relax and enjoy lunch.
  • Afternoon: Explore Agatti Island, its beaches, main jetty, local culture, and lagoon views.
  • Evening: Sunset by the beach to unwind on your first day.

Day 2:
Thinnakara Island Day Trip and Snorkeling

  • Morning: Boat ride to Thinnakara Island. Enjoy snorkeling in vibrant coral reefs.
  • Afternoon: Lunch at Thinnakara Island.
  • Evening: Return to accommodation.

Day 3:
Scuba Diving and Kalppitti Island Trip

  • Morning: Optional scuba diving (not included in package).
  • Afternoon: Relax or explore the island.
  • Evening: Glass-bottom boat trip to Kalppitti Island with stunning sunset views.

Day 4:
Departure

  • Morning: Free time for beach stroll or shopping.
  • Late Morning: Check-out and transfer to airport.
