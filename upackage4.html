Malaysia – Detailed Itinerary

Day 1
Our journey starts from Trivandrum airport.
Malaysia Airlines: TRV 01:20AM → KUL 08:10AM → LGK 09:25AM → 10:35AM (20kg Baggage + Meals)
After reaching Langkawi airport at 10:35AM, check-in to hotel. After lunch, half-day island tour to Eagle Square and Pentai Cenang beach.
Stay at Langkawi

Day 2
After breakfast, full-day island tour including Island hopping (SIC basis), Skycab, Sky Bridge, Skyglide and 3D Art Museum (Entry tickets included).
Stay at Langkawi

Day 3
Checkout from hotel and drop off at Langkawi Airport.
Malaysia Airlines: LGK 11:10AM → KUL 12:25PM (20kg Baggage + Meals)
After reaching Kuala Lumpur, tour to Putrajaya, KL Tower Observatory Deck, Petronas Twin Towers & Bukit Bintang.
Stay at Kuala Lumpur

Day 4
After check-out, visit Genting Highlands via cable car, Chin Swee Cave Temple & Batu Caves.
Drop off to airport for flight.
AirAsia: KUL 21:45 → COK 23:15 (20kg Baggage)
Journey ends here but memories never do.
