Dubai & Abu Dhabi(4 Nights 5 Days)

Day 1:

Starting our Journey from Cochin International Airport. On arrival after breakfast: BAPS Hindu Mandir (Bochasanwasi Akshar Purushottam Swaminarayan Sanstha) – Abu Dhabi's traditional Hindu temple. Inspired by Pramukh Swami Maharaj and consecrated by Mahant Swami Maharaj on 14 February 2024. In 1997, Pramukh Swami envisioned a mandir here. The UAE government allotted 27 acres of land for it in 2015.

After lunch: Abu Dhabi City tour including Grand Mosque.
Then journey to Dubai and hotel check-in.

Day 2:

After breakfast: Visit Dubai Frame, Dubai Mall, Burj Khalifa (124th floor).
Evening: Dhow Cruise with Dinner. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 3:

After breakfast: Dubai City Tour (Burj Al Arab, Palm Jumeirah, Hotel Atlantis, Zabeel Palace - outside views).
After lunch: Desert Safari with BBQ Dinner and entertainment shows (Tanoura, Belly & Fire dance). Overnight stay.

Day 4:

After breakfast: Visit Miracle Garden (72,000 sq.m with over 50 million flowers).
After lunch: Global Village – shopping, culture, concerts. Overnight stay.

Day 5:

After breakfast: Hotel checkout and shopping at Gold Souk & Spice Souk.
After lunch: Transfer to Abu Dhabi Airport. Fly back home with great memories.

NB: Passport must have at least 6 months validity.
