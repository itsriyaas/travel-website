Pattaya & Bangkok (5 Days 4 Nights)

Day 1

Departure from Nedumbasseri Airport via Thai AirAsia.

Flight: COK 23:00 → DMK 04:30 (7 kg hand luggage only)

Day 2

  • Arrival at Bangkok Airport
  • Pickup and transfer to Pattaya via Sri Racha Tiger Zoo (Breakfast & photo with tiger)
  • Check-in to hotel, Lunch
  • Sanctuary of Truth, City Tour (View Point & Big Buddha)
  • Evening: Alcazar Show
  • Dinner & stay at 4-star hotel, Pattaya

Day 3

  • Coral Island Tour (speedboat, watersports)
  • Floating Market, Terminal 21 Mall
  • Dinner & stay at 4-star hotel, Pattaya

Day 4

  • Noong Nooch Tropical Botanical Garden
  • Gems Gallery
  • Dinner & stay at 4-star hotel, Pattaya

Day 5

  • Safari World & Marine Park (Open Zoo)
  • Lunch & stay at 4-star hotel, Bangkok

Day 6

  • Train Market
  • Bangkok City Tour (Golden Buddha, Marble Temple)
  • Drop at DMK Airport
  • Flight: DMK 20:20 → COK 22:30
  • Return to Nedumbasseri Airport with unforgettable memories

Inclusions:

  • All meals (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)
  • 3-night stay in 4-star hotel – Pattaya
  • 1-night stay in 4-star hotel – Bangkok (Twin Sharing)
  • All transportation by Private AC Bus
  • Tour Guide
  • 1L bottled water per day
  • Thai Travel Accident Insurance

Exclusions:

  • Flight Tickets
  • Optional water sports like Scuba diving, Paragliding, etc.
